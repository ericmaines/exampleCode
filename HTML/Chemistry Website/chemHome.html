The Periodic Table

An amazing invention by Dmitri Mendeleev that revolutionized chemistry!

2019 is known as the International Year of the Periodic Table by UNESCO and IUPAC because it marks the 150th anniversary of its creation!

Did you know?

  • Chemists use the periodic table every day
  • The periodic table allows us to predict the properties of elements without having to hold them in our hands!
  • The version of the periodic table you see normall is not the full table! It's condensed to fit all of the elements on one page
